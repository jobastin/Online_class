Welcome


The Congregation of the Sisters of St. Aloysius was founded in the year 1815 . The founder of this religious congregation is Father Govaniti Battista Rubino . Fr Rubino was born on 12th Feb 1776 at La-morra-in Italy. Fr. Rubino died on 11th February 1853 at Lamorra- Italy . Since then the founderâ€™s day is celebrated on 11th February. St. Aloysius English Medium High School is a wonderful opportunity for any child. It is a place where students are natured as individuals and they are challenged to push their boundaries from the class room to the wider society. It is a place for tremendous academic and personal growth. A place where students learn not only how to become good students, but good citizens as well. It runs classes from LKG to X. The school gives opportunity to every child to become socially upright citizens and to develop critical thinking within a safe environment. The students of St. Aloysius are trained and equipped in every aspect to meet the challenges of the modern world. As a Catholic minority institution, St. Aloysius Senior Secondary School has for its inspiration and guidance, the life and teaching of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To inculcate in our students the values of - Love and concern for others, Friendliness and co-operation, Truthfulness, Respect for all as children of God Discipline and self control responsible citizenship . Care and concern for the environment, To promote in our students academic excellence, Leadership skills, All round development.
